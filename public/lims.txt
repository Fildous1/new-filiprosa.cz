IDENTITY
--------
Filip Rosa is a young, aspiring photographer based in Vyškov, a town in the
South Moravian Region (Jihomoravský kraj) of the Czech Republic. He focuses
primarily on analog (film) photography and provides photographic services
across South Moravia. He works with both digital and analog formats, but
analog craft — from shooting on film to darkroom printing — is the core of
his identity.


SERVICES (what Filip offers)
-----------------------------
1. Zachycení vzpomínek / Capturing memories
   Portrait, product, and event photography. Available in both digital and
   analog aesthetics. Keywords: portréty, produktová fotografie, eventová
   fotografie.

2. Vyvolávání / Film development
   Individual black-and-white film development. Quality, affordable,
   personalised service. Keywords: vyvolávání filmů, černobílé filmy.

3. Fyzické fotografie / Darkroom prints
   Handmade darkroom silver gelatin prints. Physical photographs with a
   genuine analog character. Keywords: darkroom tisky, zvětšeniny.

4. Skenování / Scanning
   High-quality scanning of negatives and slides.
   Keywords: skenování negativů, diapozitivy.


SITE STRUCTURE
--------------
The website (filiprosa.cz) is a bilingual (Czech / English) portfolio site
built with Next.js (static export), styled with Tailwind CSS, and animated
with Framer Motion. Content is served from a CDN at cdn.filiprosa.cz.

Page sections (in order, accessible via anchor links):
  /           — Homepage with all sections
  /#sluzby    — Services section (the four service cards above)
  /#projekty  — Projects overview (links to sub-pages)
  /#o-mne     — About / contact section (profile photo, two text paragraphs,
                stats: years of experience, cameras in collection, films developed)
  /#kontakt   — Contact form (name, e-mail, message)

Subpages:
  /galerie    — Photo gallery. Albums of Filip's work. Images served from CDN.
  /muzeum     — Camera museum. Filip's personal analog camera collection.
                Each camera has brand, model, year, country of origin,
                acquisition story, and sample images.
  /rosnik     — Nerovný Rosník. A self-published analog photography magazine
                (PDF download). Multiple issues.
  /mafos      — Mafoš project. A film discovery/outreach project connecting
                people with analog photography.
  /vybaveni   — Gear / equipment page. Overview of cameras and darkroom
                equipment Filip uses.
  meetup.filiprosa.cz — External site for photography meetup events.


PROJECTS
--------
Nerovný Rosník
  A magazine about analog photography created by Filip Rosa. Issues are
  available as free PDF downloads from the site. "Nerovný" roughly translates
  to "uneven / rough / raw" — reflecting the lo-fi, handmade feel.

Kamera Museum (Muzeum kamer)
  Filip's growing collection of vintage and analog cameras. The museum section
  of the site catalogs each camera with detailed metadata (type, sensor format,
  release year, country, working condition, purchase price, personal notes).

Mafoš
  A community/outreach project around analog film photography. Aimed at
  introducing people to film shooting and darkroom work.

Fotomeetup
  Regular photography meetups organised by Filip. Hosted at
  meetup.filiprosa.cz.


LOCATION & SERVICE AREA
------------------------
Home base: Vyškov, South Moravia, Czech Republic

Vyškov, Brno, and the broader South Moravian Region.


TONE & AESTHETIC
----------------
- Craft-first. Every photograph is treated as a manual, intentional act.
- Analog at heart: film grain, darkroom chemistry, physical prints.
- Minimalist, dark visual aesthetic on the site (dark backgrounds, lime/gold
  accent colour, Playfair Display serif headings, DM Sans body text).
- Bilingual: Czech is the primary language; English translation available.
- Young, approachable voice — not a corporate studio, but an individual
  craftsperson.


TECHNICAL NOTES (for AI tools reading this file)
-------------------------------------------------
- Framework: Next.js (App Router, static export)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS 4
- Animation: Framer Motion
- Fonts: Playfair Display (headings), DM Sans (body)
- i18n: client-side, Czech + English, stored in lib/i18n.tsx
- CDN: cdn.filiprosa.cz — flat-file PHP server on WEDOS shared hosting
- Admin panel: /panel0x — password-protected, manages CDN content
- No database; all content stored as JSON manifests on CDN
- Images: served from CDN, resized client-side before upload
  (full: 1920px, thumbnail: 720px, JPEG 85–88% quality)
- Contact form posts to CDN API (/api/contact), which sends email
- Sitemap: /sitemap.xml
- Robots: /robots.txt
